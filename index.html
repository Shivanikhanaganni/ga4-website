About Me

Hello, I'm Shivani! Ever since I was a child, I have been fascinated by books. They have taken me to different worlds, introduced me to new ideas, and helped me understand the world better. I started collecting books as a way to preserve these wonderful stories and share them with others. This website is my personal library where I want everyone to find something they love—whether it's fiction, science, or technology. I hope you enjoy exploring these books as much as I do!
